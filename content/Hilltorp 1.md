Hilltorp is the old elven civilization. that got destroyed by giants long ago, they terrorized hiltorp and its citizens, many people trued to fight the giants and some of them were slain. After  2 months of clashes and casualties  a great "sage,magician,wizard" invented a spell that could control the giants. the great  "sage,magician,wizard" had a brother whom was a druid they used their massive knowledge of nature and magic to rebuild a civilization. They are now know as the founders of [[Tel' Myth Ath Tel' Vandor]]. 

With the newfound newly grown tree and enslaved giants they could now build a city. Unfortunately the spell was starting to were of so with quick thinking the wizard sealed them into the walls of [[Tel' Myth Ath Tel' Vandor]]  

The giants are waiting for the walls to break so they can get their revenge on the elves.
